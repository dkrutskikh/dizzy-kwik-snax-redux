# KwikSnax Redux — Lore

## Setting

The game takes place in a cartoon fantasy world inspired by the universe of classic Dizzy series games from the early 1990s. The game world consists of small gameplay arenas that appear as parts of a larger fantastical world.

Level themes may include:

- kitchens and pantries
- castles and basements
- factories and workshops
- ice rooms
- caves and dungeons
- fairy-tale villages
- abstract gameplay spaces

The environment should feel like part of a living world, while still following arcade-style logic. Objects and enemies exist for gameplay purposes rather than realistic storytelling. The story is minimal and serves only as background for the game.

## Main Character

The main character is a cartoon-style hero inspired by Dizzy and other arcade mascots from the early 1990s.

Character requirements:

- simple and recognizable silhouette
- rounded shapes
- expressive facial animation
- clear and readable movement

The hero should look:

- friendly
- slightly naive
- energetic
- capable of getting into funny situations

The character should not have a complex backstory.

The player should immediately understand:

- who the hero is
- what the hero does
- what needs to be collected
- what must be avoided

## Supporting Characters

The game world may include characters styled similarly to inhabitants of the Dizzy universe.

Character types:

- second player (co-op hero)
- cartoon enemies
- neutral creatures
- living objects
- bonus characters

Requirements:

- characters should look friendly in style, even if they are dangerous in gameplay terms
- enemies should look funny or strange, not scary
- allies should look friendly
- bonus characters should look helpful

In cooperative play, players must be distinguishable by:

- color
- accessories
- shape details
- animations

## Tone

The overall tone of the game should match classic CodeMasters games from the early 1990s.

Main characteristics:

- light
- playful
- fairy-tale-like
- cartoonish
- slightly chaotic
- kind

The game must not feel:

- dark
- realistic
- violent
- serious
- dramatic

Defeat should look:

- funny
- quick
- non-violent

Victory should look:

- bright
- joyful
- celebratory

The lore should create the feeling that the player is inside a classic arcade world, where everything exists to support fun and fast gameplay.
